About TT&J Poultry

Our story, mission, and commitment to quality

Our Story

TT&J Poultry was founded in 2015 by three passionate poultry farmers with a vision to provide high-quality poultry products to Kenyan households and businesses. What started as a small family farm in Nairobi has grown into a trusted poultry supplier serving customers across Kenya.

Over the years, we've expanded our operations, invested in modern poultry farming techniques, and built a network of satisfied customers who rely on us for their poultry needs. Our commitment to quality, animal welfare, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh, healthy, and affordable poultry products while promoting sustainable farming practices. We aim to:

  • Deliver consistent quality in all our products
  • Support local farmers through partnerships
  • Educate our customers on best poultry practices
  • Contribute to food security in Kenya
  • Maintain high standards of animal welfare
