Delivery Information

How we get our products to you across Kenya

Our Delivery Process

At TT&J Poultry, we understand the importance of timely and safe delivery of poultry products. We've developed a reliable delivery system to serve customers across Kenya.

1

Place Your Order

Order via phone, WhatsApp, or our contact form. Provide delivery address and preferred delivery date.

2

Order Processing

We confirm availability and prepare your order with proper packaging for transport.

3

Dispatch

Your order is dispatched with appropriate temperature control for product freshness.

4

Delivery

Our delivery team brings your order to the specified location at the scheduled time.

Delivery Areas & Charges

We deliver to all major towns and cities in Kenya. Delivery charges vary based on location and order size.

Nairobi

  • Free delivery for orders above KSh 5,000
  • KSh 300 for orders below KSh 5,000
  • Same-day delivery for orders before 12pm

Other Major Towns

  • Nyandarua, Nakuru, Naivasha: KSh 800-1,200
  • Nairobi, Thika, Gilgil: KSh 1,000-1,500
  • Minimum order: KSh 3,000

Upcountry Areas

  • Delivery available to most upcountry towns
  • Charges vary by distance (KSh 1,500-3,000)
  • Weekly delivery schedules

For exact delivery charges to your location, please contact our sales team.
