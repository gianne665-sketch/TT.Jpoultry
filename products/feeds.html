Feeds & Supplements

Nutritionally balanced feeds for optimal poultry health

Quality Poultry Feeds

TT&J Poultry offers a complete range of high-quality feeds formulated by poultry nutrition experts. Our feeds are manufactured using premium ingredients to ensure your chickens get the right nutrition at every growth stage.

Feed Types

Starter Feed

  • For chicks 0-3 weeks old
  • High protein content (20-22%)
  • Small crumble size for easy eating
  • Contains essential vitamins and minerals

Grower Feed

  • For chickens 4-6 weeks old
  • Balanced protein (18-20%)
  • Promotes steady growth
  • Supports immune system development

Finisher Feed

  • For broilers 7 weeks to market
  • Optimized for weight gain (16-18% protein)
  • Enhances meat quality
  • Improves feed conversion ratio

Layer Feed

  • For egg-laying hens
  • High calcium content for strong shells
  • Balanced for consistent egg production
  • Contains omega-3 for nutritious eggs
