Professional Egg Incubation

TT&J Poultry offers state-of-the-art egg incubation services for farmers who want to hatch their own chicks without investing in expensive equipment. Our modern incubators and expert care ensure high hatch rates for your fertile eggs.

Service Features

Climate Control

Precise temperature and humidity control throughout the incubation period

Automatic Turning

Regular egg turning for proper embryo development

Candling

Regular egg examination to monitor development

Biosecurity

Strict hygiene protocols to prevent disease

Our Incubation Process

1

Egg Collection

You deliver fertile eggs to our facility or we collect them from your farm

2

Egg Selection

We carefully select and clean eggs for incubation

3

Incubation

Eggs are placed in our modern incubators for 18 days

4

Hatching

Eggs are transferred to hatchers for the final 3 days

5

Chick Collection

You collect your healthy day-old chicks

Egg Requirements

For best results, your eggs should meet these criteria:

  • Fertile (from mated flocks)
  • Less than 7 days old
  • Clean shells (not washed)
  • Normal shape and size
  • No cracks or thin spots
  • Stored at 12-15Â°C before incubation
