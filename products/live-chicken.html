Live Chicken

Healthy broilers and layers for your farm or table

Quality Live Chickens

At TT&J Poultry, we provide high-quality live chickens including broilers and layers that are healthy, well-fed, and ready for your farm or table. Our chickens are raised in optimal conditions with proper nutrition and care.

Broilers

Our broilers are fast-growing chickens bred specifically for meat production. They reach market weight in just 6-8 weeks with excellent feed conversion ratios.

Layers

Our layers are productive egg-laying hens that can produce up to 300 eggs per year with proper care. We offer various breeds suitable for different climates.

Key Features:

  • Vaccinated against common poultry diseases
  • Raised in hygienic conditions
  • Fed with quality feeds for optimal growth
  • Available in various ages and sizes
  • Delivered safely to your location
