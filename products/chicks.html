Quality Chicks for Poultry Farming

TT&J Poultry supplies high-quality day-old chicks that are vaccinated and bred for excellent growth and productivity. Our chicks come from reputable hatcheries with strict biosecurity measures.

Available Breeds

Broiler Chicks

  • Fast growth (reach market weight in 6-8 weeks)
  • Excellent feed conversion ratio
  • High meat yield

Layer Chicks

  • High egg production (280-320 eggs/year)
  • Good disease resistance
  • Adaptable to various climates

Vaccination Program

All our chicks come with the following vaccinations:

  • Marek's Disease (at hatchery)
  • Newcastle Disease
  • Infectious Bronchitis
  • Gumboro Disease

Vaccination schedule provided with each purchase.

Brooding Tips for New Chicks

Temperature

Maintain 35°C in first week, reduce by 3°C each week

Feeding

Starter feed (high protein) for first 3 weeks

Water

Clean water available at all times with electrolytes

Space

50 chicks per square meter in first week
